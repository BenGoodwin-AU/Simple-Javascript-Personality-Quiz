An Introduction to Witcher Lore

The Witcher series is most often known around its two main forms of media; the book series by Andrzej Sapkowski and the video game series by CD Projekt. The series is set inside a fantasy medieval world on the landmass known simply as The Continent. The term "Witchers" refer to people who are considered to be "mutants" who have improved their mental and physical conditioning through mysterious rituals. In additon to witchers, there are dwarves; dragons and castles; mages (known as sorcerers) and sorceresses.
